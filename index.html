JafBath Mini Project

You need to feed your Tamagotchi so they dont go hungry! But be careful as when you eat you tend to get sleepy as well.

Sleep is extremely important to the survival of your pet. Dont let this number reach 10 as your pet could faint!

Just like real pets your Tamagotchi needs exercise in order to stay happy and to decrease your boredom. But be careful as it will also make your pet more hungry!

As you age your Tamagotchi will Evolve into a stronger being. See how far you can make it!
